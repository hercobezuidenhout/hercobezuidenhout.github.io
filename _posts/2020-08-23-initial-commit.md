But, here we are. I guess.

> I will come out and say that ever since reading [Tania Rascia's](https://www.taniarascia.com) blog for the first time, I was always a tiny bit envious.

I can't say I have cool posts planned, but I might have cool posts planned. I am constantly working on new things, learning, experimenting, failing, breaking production (that is a lie actually, I have never broken production) and I would like to share these topics with you, I think. 

So, that pretty much sums up my initial commit to my blog. I do not know when I will post my next one.
